Visit the Jurassic coast

​

About

UNESCO protected coastline spanning 95 miles along southern England. Here you can explore rock pools, visit sweeping golden beaches, or go fossil hunting. There is plenty to do, from exploring the stunning coast scenery or charming seaside towns; hiking rugged paths; or visiting a range of local breweries. Top picks: the white limestone stacks of Old Harry Rocks; Corfe Castle; Lulworth Cove; Durdle Door; fossil hunting along Charmouth Beach
